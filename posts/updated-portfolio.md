Today I've finally decided that my reworked portfolio was suitable for publication. Having realized that the original design has been unfortunately usurped by most LLM's I've decided to give it an already needed update with a refreshed look.<br><br>

**<h1 style="font-family: Plus Jakarta Sans Variable; font-size: 24px; color: white">Technology</h1>**<br>

After careful consideration I've decided to stay with Next.js. The recent security breaches made me lean more and more toward a basic React implementation, but ultimately I decided to focus on other parts. Dependabot does its job well and for now I can manage with an update here and there.<br><br>

I decided to change the colors to make them less saturated and update sections where they no longer rely on card background and bright shadows. Now they get to breathe a bit more and the shadow effect is more subtle, but still noticeable. <br><br>

**<h1 style="font-family: Plus Jakarta Sans Variable; font-size: 24px; color: white">Planned Features</h1>**<br>

Keeping the **Blog** section more up to date is certainly one of them. As for other plans, I really need to wrap up and complete the implementation of **Accessibility** to work with WCAG regulations regarding keyboard navigation and screen reader cooperation, as well as **Internationalization** to support multiple languages (English/Swedish).
